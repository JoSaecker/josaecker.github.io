Welcome! I am a research assistant at the [Chair of Financial Markets and Macroeconomics](https://www.imfs-frankfurt.de/en/chairs/prof-alexander-meyer-gohde.html) and a PhD student at the [Graduate School for Economics, Finance and Management](https://www.gsefm.eu/about-us.html) at [Goethe University Frankfurt](https://www.wiwi.uni-frankfurt.de/en/home.html). I am also a member of the [Institute for Monetary and Financial Stability](https://www.imfs-frankfurt.de/en/).

My research interests are macroeconomics, economics of the green transition and numerical methods. You can find my first publication [here](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.econmod.2024.106670). 

You can find the presentation slides for the T2M Conference in Amsterdam [here](/files/042024_T2M.pdf).
